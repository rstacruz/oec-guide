POEA Memorandum Circular 08

Memorandum Circular No. 08 of 2018

This Memorandum Circular details "Guidelines on the Registration of Direct-Hire Overseas Filipino Workers."

  • POEA Memorandum Circular No. 08 of 2018.

Things to note

  • Section I
    This lists of exemptions from the direct hire ban. §I(d)(2) in particular shows that skilled workers are exempted.

  • Section II
    This covers the "employment standards" that must be met by your employment contract.

  • Section IV
    Details that Direct-Hires must undergo a Pre-Employment Medical Examination (PEME).
