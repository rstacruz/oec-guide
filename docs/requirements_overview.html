Direct hire

The process and requirements described here are for "direct hires"—that is, workers who acquired work without the intercession of a recruitment agency. As of May 2018, POEA has a ban on processing direct hire applications, and you need to prove that you're exempted as a skilled worker.

See Direct hire to understand what a "Direct Hire" is, and how to acquire an endorsement letter to prove your exemption as a skilled worker.
